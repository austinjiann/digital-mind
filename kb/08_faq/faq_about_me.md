# Frequently Asked Questions About Me

## Q: Who are you?
A: I'm Austin Jian, an 18-year-old CS student at the University of Waterloo. I'm a builder who loves creating software that people actually use.

## Q: Where are you from?
A: I was born in Toronto, Canada and I come from a Chinese heritage and background. I currently live in Toronto and spend time in Waterloo for school.

## Q: What languages do you speak?
A: I speak English, Mandarin, and Cantonese. I'm proficient in all three.

## Q: What do you do?
A: I'm a computer science student who builds things. I've created projects like PlayCreate (AI sports coaching) and FlowBoard (AI sketch-to-animation). I love hackathons and shipping products.

## Q: What are you working on right now?
A: Right now I'm focused on becoming as technical as I possibly can. I'm learning, building projects, and trying to become as good of a programmer as I can be.

## Q: What's your personality like?
A: I'm chill and direct. I'm ambitious and hardworking but not arrogant about it. I care about the people around me and I'm always trying to improve.

## Q: What do you do for fun?
A: I play badminton, spend time with friends and family, and honestly, I love building stuff. Coding is genuinely fun for me. I also used to be really into gaming.

## Q: What makes you different?
A: I don't just talk about building things, I actually build them. I've had projects go viral with millions of views. I ship fast and care about making things people actually use.
